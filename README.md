# 📊 Business Insights & Revenue Visualization

An end-to-end business analytics project using Superstore Sales data — covering data cleaning, EDA, statistical analysis, and multi-tool dashboard creation in both Power BI and Tableau.

---

## 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes 4 years of retail sales and revenue data to extract actionable business insights. It identifies high-performing product categories, regional revenue trends, the impact of discounting on profitability, and customer segment performance.

**Key Business Questions Answered:**
- Which product categories and sub-categories drive the most revenue and profit?
- How has monthly revenue and profit trended over time?
- Which regions perform best and which underperform?
- How does discounting affect profitability?
- Which customer segments generate the most value?

## 📥 Dataset

**Source:** [Superstore Sales Dataset – Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vivek468/superstore-dataset-final)

- **Size:** 9,994 orders across 4 years
- **Coverage:** USA retail, 3 product categories, 4 regions, 3 customer segments
- **Key fields:** `Order Date`, `Sales`, `Profit`, `Discount`, `Category`, `Region`, `Segment`

### How to Download:
1. Visit the Kaggle link above
2. Download and rename the file to `superstore.csv`
3. Place it in the same folder as `analysis.py`

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

- **Technology** generates the highest revenue but **Office Supplies** has a better profit margin
- **Q4** consistently shows the highest revenue across all years — seasonal spike
- Discounts above **30% almost always result in losses** — a critical pricing insight
- The **West region** leads in both revenue and profitability
- **Corporate segment** has the highest average order value; Consumer drives volume
